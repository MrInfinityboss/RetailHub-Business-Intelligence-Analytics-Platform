# Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD)

## Overview

The Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) represents the logical structure of the RetailHub Enterprise Retail Data Warehouse. It illustrates the relationships between master, transactional, inventory, logistics, marketing, and customer engagement tables.

The schema follows a normalized relational database design with clearly defined primary keys and foreign key constraints to ensure data integrity and support efficient analytical querying.

## Domains

- Product Catalog
- Inventory Management
- Sales
- Payments
- Logistics
- Marketing
- Customer Engagement
